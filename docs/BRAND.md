# Brand Notes

## Naming architecture

Current split:

- **tem-protocol** — underlying protocol / repo / builder-facing name.
- **CoffeeSip** — consumer-facing app/product brand.
- **coffeesip.app** — purchased domain on Namecheap.

Why:

- `tem-protocol` communicates the deeper thesis: Time Equals Money.
- `CoffeeSip` feels casual, social, and quick: a small paid ask, like buying someone a coffee to get a sip of their time.
- The product can feel playful and approachable while the protocol remains open and extensible.

## Product sentence

> CoffeeSip lets people pay to get your time — and can turn tiny payments into Bitcoin savings.

## Previous alternatives considered

- Tip Jar
- Time Jar
- Time Equals Money
- TEM

Current recommendation: keep `tem-protocol` for infrastructure, use **CoffeeSip** as the first app brand.
