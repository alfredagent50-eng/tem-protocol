# Brand Notes

## Naming architecture

Recommended split:

- **tem-protocol** — underlying protocol / repo / builder-facing name.
- **Tip Jar** — friendly consumer-facing app/product name.

Why:

- `tem-protocol` communicates the deeper thesis: Time Equals Money.
- `Tip Jar` is more approachable, social, and easy to explain to normal users.
- The product can feel casual and fun while the protocol remains open and extensible.

## Product sentence

> Tip Jar lets people pay to get your time — and can turn tiny payments into Bitcoin savings.

## Alternatives to consider

- Tip Jar
- Time Jar
- Time Equals Money
- TEM

Current recommendation: keep `tem-protocol` for infrastructure, use **Tip Jar** as the first app brand unless a stronger name emerges.
