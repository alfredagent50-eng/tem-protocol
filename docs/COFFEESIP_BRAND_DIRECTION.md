# CoffeeSip Brand Direction

## Locked direction for now

**S / sip / aroma**

CoffeeSip should feel minimal, communal, and premium — not cute, not crypto, not a busy startup moodboard.

The strongest mark direction is an elegant **S-shaped sip/aroma symbol**:

- reads as **sip**,
- suggests coffee steam/aroma,
- subtly implies time flow,
- works as a compact app icon,
- avoids literal clocks, coins, wallets, or crypto language.

## Voice

Use plural/communal language:

- “Our time’s worth a sip.”
- “What do we want?”
- “What should we make happen?”

Avoid apologetic explanations like “not close friends, not strangers.” The product should be self-explanatory and serious: time has value.

## Visual tone

- Very minimal 2026 product aesthetic.
- Off-white / cream canvas.
- Espresso-black text.
- Muted blue accent.
- Optional tiny amber warmth.
- Lots of whitespace.
- Rounded but not childish.
