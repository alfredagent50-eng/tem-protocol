The mark

Meridian — a bean cut by a single tilted line.

One mark. A vesica-piscis bean with a meridian at ~58°. Two visual elements. Coffee without the cup, time without the clock. Scales from billboard to favicon — with one targeted variant for tab-size where the meridian needs to thicken to survive.

Color system

Six anchors. No cream, no petrol, no amber.

Fresh palette. Oxblood is the brand primary — wine-deep, says "this handles money." Clay is the warm cousin. Signal is the 1%-use chartreuse for live moments.

Voice

Plural, present, considered.

Sip is the verb. Pour is the host's action. Slot is the unit. Don't add brew, drip, or pour-over — the lingo stays small on purpose.
