Typography

Inter for everything that has a job. Fraunces for the moments where copy is the product. Display weight is 500, never bolder — that's the editorial signature.

h4-2020/28 · -0.005em · 500
Card heading or section title goes here
body-lg-1818/28 · -0.005em · 400
Lead paragraphs and long-form articles use the larger body size — slightly tighter tracking helps it sit calmly on cream.
body-1616/24 · 0 · 400
The default body size. CoffeeSip body copy reads like an editorial column more than a SaaS dashboard, so we keep generous line-height and let lines breathe.
small-1414/20 · +0.005em · 400
Helper text, table cells, secondary metadata sit at this size with a touch of tracking for legibility.
