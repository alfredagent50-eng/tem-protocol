# Concepts not picked — 2026-05

During the brand exploration that landed on **Meridian** (now the canonical CoffeeSip brand), two other directions were drawn fully through. They live here for historical reference. Neither ships.

## `concept-1/` — Slot
A horizontal stadium (the calendar time-slot) with a single offset filled dot inside it (the booked moment). The mark *is* the product mechanic.

**Why not picked:** at favicon scale (16–32px) the mark reads as an iOS toggle switch, off-state. Suggests the brand is a UI control. Also dates if the product roadmap moves past "calendar with payment."

## `concept-2/` — Punctuation
Typographic only. `coffeesip` set in Fraunces with the dot of the lowercase `i` in `sip` replaced by an oversized filled circle in oxblood.

**Why not picked:** the dot is calibrated to one specific font, weight, and letter-spacing combination. Anyone using a fallback breaks the move. The brand becomes "Fraunces with an oxblood dot" — a thinner ownership claim.

## `concept-3/` — Meridian
This is what was picked. **Promoted** to `apps/web/public/brand/` and `docs/BRAND_KIT.md`. Not archived here.
